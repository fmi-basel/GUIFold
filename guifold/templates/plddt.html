pLDDT score

Helps to assess confidence in the local fold and to select the best output model when running a monomer prediction.

The table below lists for each predicted model the mean pLDDT score. The pLDDT for individual residues can be visualized in 3D model viewers by coloring the protein chain by B-factor. The model with the highest overall pLDDT is considered the best model. In case of multimer prediction the PAE score is more relevant and should be used to rank models.
